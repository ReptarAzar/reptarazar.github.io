About

  • My Experience

    I have had a part-time psychology practice for over 15 years in Portsmouth, RI. Having recently retired from Roger Williams University as the Director of the Counseling Center for 26 years, I am now in the process of expanding my private practice. I feel comfortable and confident with a variety of issues and concerns. Adults (18 and older) would probably find me most helpful who are struggling with relationships, anxiety, depression, grief/loss, transitions (e.g., divorce, retirement, emerging adulthood, starting families), work stress and anger management.

    While at Roger Williams University in addition to providing counseling, I trained dozens of doctoral students who are now practicing psychologists across the country. I also worked at the Veterans Administration Medical Center in Northampton, MA and at a community mental health center in Westfield, MA.

    My professional interests include the mind-body connection, yoga, mindfulness, spirituality, sports psychology, resiliency, happiness and psychotherapy from a Buddhist Perspective.
